Our Story

Limua Bakery is a well known bakery for it's products and services. Our team of qualified chefs work around the clock to meet and satisfy your needs.
We deal with a large variety of products. From pastries like cookies, marbles, black forest e.t.c to occasion cakes like birthday cakes, wedding cakes e.t.c offering them in different flavours as per your requests.
We also deal in decoration services where you can still get decoration from our store and hire a team of professional from our shop to do the decorations for you
We also offer classes for interested individuals where you learn from the best of the best.
